Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a psychological assessment involve?

An assessment typically involves clinical interviews with parents, observation of the child, and standardized testing. We look at cognitive, emotional, social, and behavioral functioning to provide a complete picture of your child's needs.

How can counseling help parents?

Parenting a child with developmental needs can be challenging. We offer support to help you manage stress, understand your child's unique way of thinking, and build a positive home environment.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict ethical guidelines regarding patient confidentiality and privacy. Your family's information is safe with us.
