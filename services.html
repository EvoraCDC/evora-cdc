Occupational Therapy

We offer evidence-based occupational therapy services for children with developmental, sensory, motor, and functional challenges. Therapy is tailored to enhance daily living skills, participation, and overall quality of life.

  • Sensory integration

We offer evidence-based behavioral therapy using Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) and other proven techniques to support positive behavior development, emotional regulation, and social skills. Our Approach

How We Work With Your Family

A collaborative, step-by-step process designed for success

01

Initial Consultation

Meet with our team to discuss your child's needs, challenges, and goals. We listen carefully to understand your family's unique situation.

02

Comprehensive Assessment

Our specialists conduct thorough evaluations to identify your child's strengths and areas for development.

03

Personalized Plan

We create a customized treatment plan with specific, measurable goals tailored to your child's needs.

04

Active Therapy

Begin regular sessions with our expert therapists using evidence-based techniques in a supportive environment.

05

Progress Tracking

Regular assessments and parent communications to monitor development and adjust strategies as needed.

06

Ongoing Support

Continuous guidance, parent training, and adjustments to ensure long-term success and growth.
