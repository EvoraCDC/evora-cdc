Common Questions About Our Services

Quick answers to help you learn more about Evora

We offer a comprehensive range of services including Speech Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Behavioral Therapy, Psychological Services, Counseling, Preschool Training Program (Ability School), and Autism Support. Each service is tailored to meet the unique needs of every child.

You can schedule a consultation by calling us at +91-8848481283, filling out the contact form above, or sending us a WhatsApp message. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours to arrange a convenient time.

We serve children from birth through 12 years of age. Our programs are specifically designed for different age groups: Early Intervention (0-3 years), Preschool (3-5 years), and School Age (6-12 years).

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us with your insurance information, and our administrative team will verify your coverage and benefits.

Session lengths vary depending on the type of therapy and your child's needs. Typically, sessions range from 30 to 60 minutes. During your consultation, we'll create a customized schedule that works best for your child.
