Preschool Training Program

Ability School: Building Foundations for Academic Success

Overview

Specialized Early Education

Ability School is our specialized preschool training program designed to develop foundational skills and school readiness. We provide a structured yet nurturing environment where children attain the social, academic, and behavioral skills necessary for mainstream schooling. Our curriculum is adapted to meet individual learning styles and needs.

School Readiness

Preparing children for the routine, social interactions, and learning environment of a regular classroom.

Academic Support

Building early literacy and numeracy skills through engaging, multi-sensory activities.

Individualized Education Plans

Tailored goals and strategies for each child to ensure they are learning at their own pace.

Learning Disabilities

Specialized support for children who may have specific learning difficulties or developmental delays.

A Bridge to Mainstream Education

We believe every child deserves the chance to succeed in school. Our program bridges the gap between early intervention and formal schooling, ensuring a smoother transition for your child.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is this program for?

Our Preschool Training Program is generally designed for children aged 2 to 6 years who need additional support before transitioning to a regular school environment.

Is this a full-time school?

We offer flexible timing options. Please contact us to discuss the specific schedule that would best suit your child's needs.

Will my child be able to join a regular school later?

Yes, that is our primary goal. We work on the specific skills needed for inclusion in mainstream schools, and we support the transition process when your child is ready.
