Ability School

Preschool Training Program

Our Preschool Training Program, known as Ability School, is designed to provide specialized early education for children who need additional support in their developmental journey. We create a nurturing environment where every child can learn, grow, and develop essential skills for school readiness.

Through play-based learning and individualized attention, we help children build confidence, develop social skills, and prepare for the transition to formal schooling. Our experienced educators work closely with families to ensure each child receives the support they need to thrive.

What We Offer

01

Play-Based Learning

Children learn best through play. Our curriculum incorporates engaging activities that make learning fun while building essential cognitive and motor skills.

02

Social Skills Development

We focus on helping children develop positive peer interactions, communication skills, and emotional regulation in a supportive group setting.

03

School Readiness

Our program prepares children for the academic and social demands of formal schooling, building foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and classroom behavior.

04

Individualized Support

Each child receives personalized attention based on their unique learning style, strengths, and areas for growth.

05

Family Partnership

We work closely with parents, providing regular updates and strategies to support learning at home.

06

Safe Environment

Our facility is designed to be safe, accessible, and welcoming, creating the perfect space for children to explore and learn.
